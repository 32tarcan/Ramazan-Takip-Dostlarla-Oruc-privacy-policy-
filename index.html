Privacy Policy - Ramazan Takip: Dostlarla Oruç

Last Updated: February 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ramazan Takip: Dostlarla Oruç. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Information: Email and authentication data through Firebase
  • User Profile Data: Name and promo code
  • Usage Data: Fasting status, completed challenges, and points
  • Friend Connections: Friend lists and their public profile information
  • Technical Information: Device type, operating system version

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide fasting tracking and social features
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Enable friend connections through promo codes
  • Track challenge completion and points
  • Maintain and improve app functionality
  • Send important updates about the service

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase and protected by industry-standard security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

5. Subscription Data

For subscription management, we use:

  • RevenueCat for subscription processing
  • Apple's App Store payment system

Payment information is handled securely through Apple's payment system.

6. Data Sharing

We share limited data with:

  • Friends you connect with through promo codes (only public profile information)
  • Service providers (Firebase, RevenueCat) that help us operate the app
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Manage your subscription preferences
  • Control your friend connections

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app.
